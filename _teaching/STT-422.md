[Download Syllabus](https://shrijitabhattacharya.github.io/files/STT_422_Syllabus.pdf)

STT 422 is the undergraduate course covering fundamentals of stastical estimation and inference along with their implementation in [RStudio](https://rstudio-education.github.io/hopr/starting.html). Topics covered include:
* Confidence interval and P-values.
* Z-tests, T-tests and Chi-square tests.
* One-way and two-way analysis of variance (ANOVA).
* Linear regression and logistic regression.
* Rank-tests.
* Bootstrap methods.

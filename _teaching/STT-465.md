STT 465 is the undergraduate course covering fundamentals of Bayesian statistical methods along with their implementation in [RStudio](https://rstudio-education.github.io/hopr/starting.html). Topics covered include:
* Bayes theorem and Conjugate distributions.
* Monte Carlo methods.
* Normal models and Gibbs sampling.
* Bayesian linear regression and model selection.
* Metropolis Hastings algorithm.
* Bayesian logistic regression and model selection.
